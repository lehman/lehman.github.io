About Me

My name is Louise Lehman. I am interested in 3D Modeling, Rigging, and Animation, Web Design, Computer Graphics, and making games. That is to say, I am interested in making things that interest you. I am a recent graduate from UCLA's engineering school with a B.S. in Computer Science. I've also taken classes under UCLA's Design and Media Arts school and Film and Television school, in the animation graduate program. Since graduating, I have participated in my first Game Jam, run two half marathons with my dad, produced work in Photoshop and Unity (I'm trying to edge my way into the creative realm), and read books that weren't textbooks. I aspire to some day work in the entertainment industry, enabling others to bring the creations in their mind to reality, and encouraging and inspiring the imagination of others.
