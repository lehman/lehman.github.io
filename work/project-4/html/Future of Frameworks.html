Future of Web Frameworks

A Quick Look Back

This timeline visually reveals that multiple new frameworks are created each year. There isn't one framework that is good for everything. That's why people continue to make new frameworks and improvements on old ones every year.

Looking Forward

Future frameworks will only survive if they satisfy most, if not all, of the following:

  • have good site speed
  • support plugins
  • build on past successes
  • are both conservative and edgy
  • encourage new thinking
  • support mobile, touch screen, and desktop
